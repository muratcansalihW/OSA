About
OSA Team

The founders of the Open Soccer Alliance intentionally chose the word “Alliance” to reflect the desired nature and vision of this entity.

OSA intends to unite likeminded clubs and leagues throughout the United States who share the desire to play soccer in an open system in the way the game is structured in the rest of the world.

The mission of OSA shares much of the new non-profit United Soccer America’s mission. USA is a partner of OSA and its mission is to unite and grow American soccer into the nation’s preeminent sport by championing the beautiful game’s universal principles of sporting merit, solidarity, promotion and relegation, and subsidiarity across all 50 states.
